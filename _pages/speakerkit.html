The speaker info kit provides conference and event planners with essential information about the speaker. Below are things to consider when hiring me for a talk:

  • Introduction statement before coming on stage can be found here.
  • Audio/Video Requirements
      Wireless lapel/lavalier microphone
      Audio from slides playing through venue sound system
      Slide advancer/clicker
      Slides will be provided to the AV team to run on their laptop
      Projector and screen suitable for the size of the audience
  • Technical requirements:
      Reliable internet connection (if live demo is part of the talk)
      Power outlet near the stage for laptop charging
      HDMI cable for the projector
